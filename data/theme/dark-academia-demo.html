Essential Reading List

A curated selection of works that have shaped intellectual discourse through the ages.

01

The Republic

Plato, trans. Benjamin Jowett

A foundational text of Western philosophy, exploring justice, the ideal state, and the nature of the philosopher-king.

02

Meditations

Marcus Aurelius, trans. Gregory Hays

Personal writings of the Roman Emperor, offering timeless wisdom on stoic philosophy and the art of living.

03

The Divine Comedy

Dante Alighieri, trans. John Ciardi

An epic journey through Hell, Purgatory, and Paradise, allegorizing the soul's ascent to the divine.

04

The Brothers Karamazov

Fyodor Dostoevsky, trans. Richard Pevear

A profound exploration of faith, doubt, and morality through the lives of three very different brothers.
